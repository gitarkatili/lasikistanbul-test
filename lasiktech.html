Our Advanced LASIK Technology

At EyeSTAR, we invest in the world's most advanced, precise, and safest technology to ensure you get the best possible results.

WaveLight EX500 Excimer Laser

At EyeSTAR LASIK Institute, we use the WaveLight EX500 Excimer Laser, one of the most advanced and reliable systems in the world of refractive surgery. Built on the trusted WaveLight platform, this laser operates at an incredible 500 Hz, meaning it checks and rechecks the position of your eye 500 times per second during treatment. This level of precision ensures that each laser pulse is perfectly placed—even if your eye moves slightly.

What does that mean for you? Faster, safer procedures with pinpoint accuracy. The average treatment time is just 1.4 seconds per diopter, which means less time under the laser and a smoother experience overall. The system’s swivel bed design also minimizes the time between flap creation and laser treatment, which helps maintain corneal hydration and improves outcomes.

FEMTO LDV Z6 Femtosecond Laser

We use the FEMTO LDV Z6 femtosecond laser—an exceptional innovation in laser eye surgery that allows us to perform LASIK with even greater precision, safety, and comfort. Unlike older femtosecond lasers, the Z6 uses ultra-fast, low-energy light pulses to create the corneal flap—the crucial first step of LASIK.

What sets the FEMTO LDV Z6 apart is its gentle yet incredibly fast approach. It delivers pulses at a rate of 5 million per second, using less than 10% of the energy of conventional femtosecond lasers. This results in a safer, more comfortable experience and supports faster visual recovery.

  • Enhanced Safety: Lower energy means less discomfort, reduced inflammation, and decreased light sensitivity after surgery.
  • Greater Precision: The Z6 allows our surgeons to create highly accurate corneal flaps—especially important for patients with high prescriptions or thinner corneas.
  • Faster Visual Recovery: Thanks to its overlapping bubble pattern, the treatment surface is exceptionally smooth—leading to quicker healing and clearer vision in a shorter time.
  • Lower Risk of Dry Eye: The Z6’s gentle energy delivery reduces disturbance to the eye's surface, lowering the chance of post-surgery dry eye symptoms.

The WaveLight Allegretto Wave Eyetracker

The EyeSTAR eyetracker is an integral part of EyeSTAR LASIK treatment. It works with the laser for an ultra-precise treatment, ensuring that each laser pulse is placed exactly where it should be.

EyeSTAR's eyetracker has a sampling rate faster than 500Hz and it follows eye movements 500 times per second. This is critical because the eye can twitch involuntarily. If the eyetracker is too slow, the laser may not receive the correct positioning information.

What is also extremely important is the ability of the laser to react to that information. The EyeSTAR laser and eyetracker both work at 500Hz, which means that as the eyetracker is following the eye 500 times per second, the laser is working at the same speed, precisely ablating the necessary areas of the cornea based on real-time data.

The WaveLight Allegro Topolyzer

The basis for successful eye laser treatment is an accurate diagnosis. The EyeSTAR ALLEGRO Topolyzer is an ideal device to support the decision-making process for customized laser vision correction.

With an extraordinarily high resolution of the cornea resulting from 22,000 measuring points, the ALLEGRO Topolyzer is an accurate tool for preparing refractive surgery. It produces a refractive map to display aberrations and is fitted with an interactive elevation map, allowing for individual selection of reference body, radius and sphericity.

The WaveLight Allegro T-CAT Treatment

The T-CAT software has been specifically designed to measure and generate high-resolution treatment data for eyes with severe irregularities. It was developed for the most difficult cases, in which standard wavefront measurement would prove ineffective. All necessary parameters are transferred from the Topolyzer to the laser, which then calculates the appropriate correction pattern.

The WaveLight Allegro Analyzer

The EyeSTAR ALLEGRO Analyzer is an ideal diagnostic device to support the decision-making process for customized laser vision correction. It greatly enhances the range of possible treatments, from the standard wavefront-optimized procedure to the highly sophisticated wavefront-guided treatment (A-CAT).

ALLEGRO A-CAT treatments are intended for use on patients with a high incidence of high-order aberrations in their optical system. Any required correction to the optical path of the eye is then enabled via the A-CAT module on the cornea.

Tscherning Principle

Working in the visible spectrum, the ALLEGRO Analyzer makes wavefront measurements by utilizing the Tscherning operating principle. An image of measurement spots is projected onto the retina, allowing the physician to constantly verify that the image has been taken properly and that all measurement spots are valid. This minimizes the risk of false treatment data from local obstructions.
