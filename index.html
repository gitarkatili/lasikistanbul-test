Pioneering Technology for Unmatched Precision

At EyeSTAR, your vision is our top priority. That's why we use the WaveLight® EX500 Excimer Laser, one of the most advanced and fastest laser systems available. This state-of-the-art, FDA-approved technology allows for a procedure that is not only exceptionally fast but also incredibly precise and safe.

  • Superior Speed: Minimizes treatment time for your comfort and reduces the risk of corneal dehydration.
  • Pinpoint Accuracy: Multi-dimensional eye-tracking ensures the laser is perfectly centered, delivering optimal results even with microscopic eye movements.
  • Customized Treatment: Wavefront Optimized® technology preserves the natural shape of your cornea, leading to sharper, higher quality vision.

Ideal candidates are over 18, have had a stable prescription for at least one year, and have good overall eye health. Conditions like myopia, hypermetropia, and astigmatism can be treated. The best way to know for sure is to book your free, no-obligation consultation.
