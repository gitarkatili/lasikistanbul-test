Your Questions, Answered

Find answers to common questions about LASIK.

No, the procedure is not painful. We use powerful anesthetic eye drops to completely numb your eye before and during the procedure. You may feel a slight pressure sensation, but most patients report no pain at all.

Recovery is remarkably fast. Most patients notice significantly improved vision within hours and are able to return to work and most normal activities the very next day. We'll provide a detailed post-operative care plan.

Ideal candidates are over 18, have had a stable prescription for at least one year, and have good overall eye health. Conditions like myopia, hypermetropia, and astigmatism can be treated. The best way to know for sure is to book your free, no-obligation consultation.

You don't need to worry about blinking. We use a small, gentle device to hold your eye open. Our advanced laser systems also have eye-tracking technology that follows your eye's microscopic movements, ensuring the laser is perfectly placed at all times.
